# 5. SERVICES PAGE

## Page Objective

Clearly define services in structured professional format.

---

# 5.1 Audit & Compliance Services

### ISM & ISPS Audits

Comprehensive internal audits ensuring compliance with International Safety Management and International Ship and Port Facility Security standards.

### Environmental Compliance Audits

Assessment of vessel adherence to environmental regulations and pollution prevention protocols.

### Engine Sailing Audits

Evaluation of engineering operational efficiency and compliance during sea passages.

### Navigation Assessments

Professional evaluation of navigational procedures, passage planning, and bridge resource management.

### Navigation Audits (Static & Dynamic)

Detailed analysis of navigational practices both during active sailing and alongside operations.

### Bunkering Audits

Supervision and compliance verification of bunkering operations to minimize operational risk.

### Mooring & Anchoring Audits

Assessment of deck operations, safety practices, and risk mitigation during port operations.

### Cargo Operations Audits

Inspection and evaluation of cargo handling procedures ensuring safety and regulatory compliance.

---

# 5.2 Inspection Preparation Services

### SIRE 2.0 Pre-Vetting Preparation

Structured preparation for OCIMF SIRE 2.0 inspections, including crew readiness and documentation review.

### RightShip Inspection Preparation

Operational readiness and risk mitigation strategies aligned with RightShip assessment frameworks.

### Pre-USCG Inspection

Preparation and compliance guidance for United States Coast Guard inspections.

### Pre-PSC Inspection

Vessel preparation for Port State Control inspections to reduce deficiencies and detention risk.

---

# 5.3 Training & Crew Development

### Onboard Training

Practical onboard training sessions focusing on safety culture, compliance discipline, and operational excellence.

### Crew Development Programs

Structured mentoring to enhance navigational standards and regulatory awareness.
